Tired of losing hours to mindless scrolling? Take back control with Mindful.

Mindful isn’t just another screen-time tracker—it’s your personal digital detox coach, built to help you resist addictive feeds, reduce screen time, and reclaim your focus—without spying on you.

100% Private & Offline

  • No ads. No trackers. No cloud. Everything stays on your device—guaranteed by open-source transparency.

Stop Distractions Before They Start

  • Block Reels, Shorts, and infinite scroll loops
  • Restrict access to NSFW or time-wasting websites—even mid-load
  • Cut daily screen time by up to 30% in just one week

Smart Tools for Real Change

  • Group similar apps (e.g., all social media) and set unified limits
  • Schedule app access or bedtime mode with auto Do Not Disturb
  • Pause & reschedule notifications—past alerts saved privately for a year
  • Invincible Mode: Lock all settings except during a 10-minute weekly window
  • Parental controls with biometric lock to prevent tampering

Unlike other “focus” apps that sell your data or bombard you with ads, Mindful respects your privacy by design. Built for students, professionals, and anyone tired of digital overload.

✨ Open-source | Offline-first | No permissions overreach

Ready to break the cycle? Download Mindful today—and start living with more presence, peace, and purpose.
