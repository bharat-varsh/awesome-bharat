# Awesome Bharat — Vision & Scope

## Mission Statement

Awesome Bharat is a curated discovery platform that celebrates remarkable things made or led by Indians — across every domain imaginable. It exists to break the notion that India has nothing noteworthy, to bring into public limelight even the smallest genuine effort, and to inspire people to act — by contributing, collaborating, promoting, or starting something of their own.

The site is a **launchpad, not a destination**. Every page sparks interest, then gets out of the way — sending visitors to the actual content, the actual people, the actual work.

---

## Core Philosophy

1. **Discover, then act.** Every detail page has one primary CTA above the fold. The CTA is specific to the content type — not a generic "Learn more."
2. **Curated, not exhaustive.** Quality over quantity. Each entry is personally vetted by the curator. Not everything qualifies — only things that are genuinely interesting, impressive, or impactful.
3. **Celebrate the maker.** Every piece of content is linked back to the person or organization behind it. The maker gets the spotlight.
4. **Spark interest, then get out of the way.** The site is a teaser + action portal. 150–400 words max per entry. Enough to hook, then a clear path forward.
5. **Inspire action beyond the screen.** The Opportunities section on domain pages shows where India needs work, who's already trying, and how visitors can help — volunteer, collaborate, donate, promote, or start something new.
6. **Breadth over depth (initially).** Cover as many domains and content types as possible before going deep on any single one.

---

## What Awesome Bharat IS

- A movement-building platform celebrating Indian achievements across ALL domains
- A curated directory with editorial voice and visual energy
- A dual-navigation system: browse by domain (Space, Health, Environment) OR by content type (Apps, People, Channels)
- A call-to-action engine that drives traffic, visibility, and social media discussion to Indian creators and initiatives
- An inspiration machine: people come here, discover amazing work, and feel compelled to talk about it, support it, or start something

---

## What Awesome Bharat is NOT

- ❌ Not an encyclopedia or Wikipedia — entries are teasers, not comprehensive articles
- ❌ Not a social network — no user accounts, no profiles, no login
- ❌ Not monetized — no ads, no sponsored listings, no paid placements, no affiliate revenue
- ❌ Not user-generated content — all entries are curator-vetted, no public submission form
- ❌ Not an API or data service — no public API, no data exports
- ❌ Not limited to tech — spans arts, science, sports, social impact, environment, defense, space, culture, policy, and everything else

---

## Hard Boundaries

These decisions are permanent and non-negotiable:

| Rule | Rationale |
|------|-----------|
| No user accounts | Keeps the site simple, static, and privacy-respecting |
| No advertisements | Preserves editorial independence and trust |
| No monetization | This is a public good, not a business |
| No user-generated content | Curation quality requires editorial control |
| No paid/sponsored listings | Every entry earns its place on merit alone |
| No public API | The site is for humans, not scrapers |
| No paywalls or gated content | Everything is free and accessible |

## Target Audience

| Audience | What they get |
|----------|---------------|
| **Indian developers & tech community** | Discover Indian open-source, apps, and tools worth using and contributing to |
| **General Indian internet users** | Learn about incredible Indian achievements they never knew about |
| **Global audience curious about India** | A curated window into what India is building across every field |
| **Creators & makers themselves** | Visibility, backlinks, and traffic to their work |
| **People who want to contribute to India's growth** | Opportunities, organizations to join, initiatives to support |

---

## Success Definition

A year from now, Awesome Bharat is a success if:

1. **Breadth**: Covers 10+ content types across 15+ domains with 500+ quality entries
2. **Traffic driver**: Measurably sends visitors to listed creators' websites, channels, and repositories
3. **Discussion catalyst**: Entries from the site get shared on social media, sparking conversations about Indian achievements
4. **Inspiration engine**: People discover the site, get inspired, and either support listed initiatives or start their own
5. **Action bridge**: The Opportunities section motivates real-world action — volunteering, donations, collaborations, new projects
6. **Recognition**: Featured by tech newsletters, shared in communities, referenced by creators as a source of pride

---

## The Bigger Picture

The ultimate aspiration is to enable a positive cycle:

```
Discover → Get inspired → Visit/Support/Promote → Talk about it on social media
    → Attract more attention → More people discover → More people act
        → Leaders collaborate → Corporates and governments notice
            → Real change happens → India becomes better
```

Awesome Bharat is the spark. The community carries the flame.
